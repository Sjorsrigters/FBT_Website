Founded by Two is a graphic design and art direction studio working on commissioned and self-initiated projects. We design digital and printed media such as posters and publications, visual identities, websites, moving images and installations. Our practice seeks to form close-relationships with artists, designers, curators, publishers, cultural organisations and institutions. If you want to have a cup of tea with us or have any questions, feel free to contact us by mail or phone.
