Who we are
Founded by Two is a multidisciplinary graphic design studio founded in 2020 by Fabiola van den Berg and Sjors Rigters, two graphic designers who will graduate in the summer of 2021 from the Royal Academy of Art, The Hague.

Together the two studied graphic design for sixteen years and therefore became native speakers in visual language. While studying together they found out that they share the same love for design and typography. In the end, they are two friends who started a studio together, love playing chess and experience love at first sight with beautifully designed publications.
↓
What we do
Founded by Two is an visual identity and editorial design practice. The two produce concepts, research and many different types of digital and print media such as publications, posters, websites, videos and installations for clients in both the cultural and commercial field.

Together they approach each project as a new story to tell, with a desire to develop bold visual languages and profound design solutions, always adapted to the needs of each project. Their process-driven work often consist of an exstensive (visual) research, a feeling for image making and an happy accident once in a while, with the use of various techniques and programs.

If you have a design question, challenge or would like to collaborate feel free to contact us by phone or mail.

How It Really Happend
For an assignment from Bart de Baets, we as a class had to redesign a book about mythology. Together all of these pages would join and make the publication 'How It Really Happened'.

Sjors got the first pages of the mythology book so he decided to collect different images of eggs and make a visual narrative. These eggs represent the beginning of the publication as eggs can also represent the beginning of a life. Fabiola took one sentence from the original publication that was presented by Bart, 'God of light Helios saw everything and knew everything'. It made her think of how we see celebrities nowadays that sometimes it seems like we don't think of them as real people. Everyone can search up the weirdest things about celebrities. The result of these pages is a collection of google searches in combination with the original images from the mythology book.
