Dummy of
Two
Two friends just got out of the Royal Academy of Art The Hague after a full day of working, the sun is setting. While they walk towards the train station, they walk past Spar the supermarket which has their favourite snack, fake M&M’s (which if you haven’t tried them you should). One of them can’t wait to eat them so begins eating them while they walk to the train station. While they wait for the train to leave they talk about a future together as a design duo. At this point, they are both graphic design students about to graduate from the Royal Academy of Art. These two crossed paths because of their love for design and typography.

The train left the station and they hear someone playing music and not using headphones, Justin Bieber is playing, which seemed odd. The duo finds this really annoying but they love Justin Bieber so they ignore it. The train conductor says: Station Delft, Station Delft, al lot of people leave the train. They continued talking about their future studio, they describe it as a magical place, a place with a ping pong table and a tea bar instead of a coffee bar. They have a couple minutes left before they separete, because of them always gets out at Schiedam central. One of the bags of fake M&M’s is almost empty. They hear over the speaker “next stop Schiedam centrum” one of them gets up and the other one asks what should we call our studio? They both go quiet and that is how their day ends.

Founded by Two is a graphic design and art direction studio working on commissioned and self-initiated projects. We design digital and printed media such as posters and publications, visual identities, websites, moving images and installations. Our practice seeks to form close-relationships with artists, designers, curators, publishers, cultural organisations and institutions.
